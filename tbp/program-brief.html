The problem

Most early-stage founders fail not because their idea was bad — but because they never tested whether it was good. They build before they understand. They pitch before they’ve validated. Start Studio: ID interrupts that pattern.

What it is

Start Studio: ID is TechTown Detroit’s ideation and discovery program for pre-product founders. Working alongside assigned coaches and mentors, founders develop and validate their business model using the Lean Canvas framework — building their pitch deck progressively, one phase at a time.

How advancement works

The program is structured around three phase gates — Problem, Solution, and Strategy. Each gate requires a founder to pitch specific deck sections in a checkpoint session and receive sign-off before advancing. The Problem gate is at week 6, signed off by coach and customer discovery expert. The Solution gate is at week 12, requiring two coaches to validate the product roadmap and technical feasibility. The Strategy gate completes the Lean Canvas — not perfect, but logically constructed and defensible. The final two weeks are dedicated to synthesis, rehearsal, and defense preparation.

Quarterly clock
Intake path

Applications accepted year-round. Cohorts open quarterly in February, May, and August — 10–15 founders per batch. Founders are interviewed, placed, and onboarded before entering the progress track.

Always-on clock
Progress path

Biweekly checkpoint sessions drive accountability. Coaches work with founders toward three phase gates. Advancement is evidence-based — phase gates are pitched, not just reported. Founders build their pitch deck progressively throughout.

Monthly clock
Public path

Monthly lectures open to the broader community build a visible culture of entrepreneurship and serve as the program’s primary recruitment funnel.

What founders build
01

A pitch deck built progressively — one phase at a time, not all at the end

02

Customer discovery evidence with a coach-assigned interview target, validated for their market type

03

A defensible Lean Canvas, presented and blessed at the Strategy gate before a panel of investors and ecosystem leaders

Why it matters

Detroit’s next generation of tech entrepreneurs needs more than ideas — it needs founders who have done the work to understand whether their ideas are real. Start Studio: ID builds that discipline into the earliest stage of the founder journey, before capital is committed and before product is built.
