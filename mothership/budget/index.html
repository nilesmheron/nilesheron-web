Base annual cost is the line's normative yearly value. Each year's % modifier adjusts it. A modifier of 0 means "same as base"; −100% means "nothing that year"; +10% means "10% more than base" (or than prior year, if compound).

Compound: Y2 applies on top of Y1's dollar value, Y3 applies on top of Y2's. Good for salaries (3%/yr raises) or anything growing steadily. Off (independent): each year's modifier applies directly to the base. Good for partial-year starts, one-time costs, or anything that doesn't compound.

All fields are editable. Click name, note, base, or modifier to edit. Click × to delete a line. Use "Add line" below the table.

Edits persist in this browser only (not synced). Catalyst Fund tier dropdown swaps tier-specific base and modifiers.
