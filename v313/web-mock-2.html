[ ABOUT V313 ]

A $10M commitment.
Three partners.
One continuum.

Venture 313 is a three-year commitment from the Gilbert Family Foundation to give Detroit founders access to capital, coaching, and community. One application, routed to the right partner — TechTown for non-dilutive grants, Invest Detroit Ventures for equity, Detroit Development Fund for debt — across every stage a founder actually needs capital.
